AI-Powered Production: The Mahanagar Gas Campaign

Feb 2, 2025JustVideo Labs

We helped Mahanagar Gas make their TV ads using AI. Directors could see AI backgrounds on screen while filming. Our tools filled in missing parts of shots and matched voice dubbing perfectly.

What we built

An end-to-end on-set AI pipeline: generative previz, AI-driven environment plates rendered to LED walls, frame-extension nodes, and a custom dub-and-lipsync stack - all integrated with the existing edit workflow.

The result was a campaign that ships faster, costs less in post, and gives the director live creative control on set.
